Kamloops office / First-time printer setup / Windows & Mac

Print to the office printer.

How to add the Kamloops office printer to your computer the first time. You only do this once. After that, the printer is always there when you print.

The printer Epson WorkForce Pro WF-3820
Where Kamloops office
Time needed Under 2 minutes
Works on Windows · Mac · iPhone / iPad
If you only read this

The printer is already on the office network, so your computer can find it by itself. You don't type any numbers or install anything from a CD.

Open your computer's printer settings, click Add a printer, wait a few seconds, pick EPSON WF-3820 Series when it pops up, and click Add. That's the whole thing.

01
One quick check

Before you start. Just one thing.

For your computer to find the printer, it has to be on the same Wi-Fi network as the printer: Paradigm Corporate.

✓
Connect to the Paradigm Corporate Wi-Fi Paradigm work laptops are on Paradigm Corporate by default, so you probably don't need to do anything. If your Wi-Fi shows Paradigm Employee or a guest network instead, switch it to Paradigm Corporate first, or the printer won't show up. If you don't have the Paradigm Corporate password, ask IT.
02
The setup

Add the printer. Pick your computer.

Follow the column that matches your computer. Windows on the left, Mac on the right.

On Windows

Windows 10 or 11 (most Paradigm laptops).

  1. Click the Start button (the Windows flag, bottom-left of the screen), type the word Printers, and click Printers & scanners when it appears.
  2. At the top, next to Add a printer or scanner, click the Add device button. A short list begins to load.
  3. Wait about 10 seconds for the printer to show up in the list. It shows up as EPSON WF-3820 Series  or  EPSON9EB4A8 (WF-3820 Series)
  4. Click Add device next to it. Windows installs everything it needs. This can take up to a minute, so leave it alone until it finishes.
  5. When it stops and the printer appears in your list, you're finished. ✓ Done

On a Mac

Any modern MacBook or iMac.

  1. Click the Apple menu (the apple in the very top-left corner) and choose System Settings.
  2. In the list on the left, scroll to and click Printers & Scanners.
  3. Click the Add Printer, Scanner, or Fax… button. A window opens and begins searching.
  4. Wait a few seconds, then click the printer when it appears in the list. It shows up as EPSON WF-3820 Series  or  EPSON9EB4A8 (WF-3820 Series)
  5. Leave everything as-is and click Add. Your Mac sets it up on its own. ✓ Done

Print a test page to be sure

Open any document, press Ctrl + P (Windows) or ⌘ + P (Mac), choose EPSON WF-3820 Series from the printer list, and click Print. If a page comes out, you're all set.

On an iPhone or iPad? There's nothing to install. Just open what you want to print, tap the Share icon, tap Print, and pick EPSON WF-3820 Series, as long as you're on the Paradigm Corporate Wi-Fi.

03
If something's off

Quick fixes. Try these first.

The printer never shows up in the list
Three things, in order: (1) make sure you're on the Paradigm Corporate Wi-Fi, not Paradigm Employee or guest (see Section 01). (2) Turn the printer off for 30 seconds, then back on. This fixes most issues. (3) Close the Add Printer window and open it again to make it search fresh.
It says “offline” or won't print
Almost always a quick power-cycle fixes it: switch the printer off, wait 30 seconds, switch it on, and try printing again. Give it a minute to wake up and reconnect.
Pages come out blank, faded, or streaky
That's an ink or print-head issue, not a network one. On the printer's own screen, run Maintenance → Print Head Nozzle Check, and check the ink levels. Let IT know if it keeps happening.
I see two printers with similar names
Pick the one named EPSON WF-3820 Series. You can remove any duplicate later from the same Printers settings screen. It won't cause harm in the meantime.
